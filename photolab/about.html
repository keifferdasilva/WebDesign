About Me

My name is Keiffer. I'm a photographer based out of Provo Utah for special events, mostly prom and other high school dances. I love to take photos because it's a way to take special moments and make them last forever.

Besides taking photos, I love to play soccer, relax by watching TV, and spend time with those that I love.

I've been taking photos since middle school and started doing prom photos when I went to prom myself.

I'd love to be a part of your special moments and immortalize them forever.

Thanks for looking into my work here at Keiffer da Silva Photography!
